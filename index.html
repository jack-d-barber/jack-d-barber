ABOUT ME

I'm a PhD student and Glasgow native currently based at the University of Aberdeen.

My goal is to combine my expertise in antimicrobial resistance (AMR) and my passion for dismantling systemic inequalities to answer research questions that go unasked. I want to use my quantitative background and my unique blend of experience, skills and enthusiasm to ask questions like:

  • which communities are disproportionately at risk of AMR?
  • why do these groups face this disproportionate risk?
  • what can we do to tackle the structural inequalities driving the above?
